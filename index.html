Counselling is not about "fixing" yourself as much as it is about "finding" yourself. It is about figuring out what works and what doesn't for you. It is about empowering yourself and relying on your strengths. It is about being patient with yourself as much as you are with others. It is about falling down and learning different ways to get back up. It is about everything you already are, and everything you want to be. It is all about you. And me? I am here to help you find yourself, what works for you, what inspires you. I am here to provide you a supportive, encouraging, non-judgmental and safe place. I am just the sidekick. YOU are the hero.

While starting counselling can feel overwhelming, it does not have to be! Our counselling relationship is just as important as the work we would do together, and it is important for you to get a better sense of whether it would be the right fit for you. In that spirit, I offer free first consultation/assessment session so we can figure out together whether we would work well together.
